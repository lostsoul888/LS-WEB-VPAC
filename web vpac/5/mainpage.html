About Us

 

 

 

 

 

Hello everyone,we are Pets Animal Clinic,knows as

VPAC

Vets for Pets Animal Clinic (VPAC) is an vetorinary clinic.Our clinic provides multiple service such as surgical,dental care and the list goes on.We understand that pets are one of your family members too.Thus,we treat them gentle with the same love.

 

 

 

 

Our Service

 

WELLNESS EXAMINATION

We provide consultation for small animals with the support of physical examination and necessary diagnostic machines such as in-house blood check, X-ray and ultrasound imaging, microscopic examination and etc.

 

SURGERY PROCEDURES

We provide an extensive list of neutering, soft tissue and orthopaedic surgeries.

 

 

VACCINATIONS AND PARASITE CONTROL

Vaccination that we are providing for dog including 6 in 1, 9 in 1, 10 in 1, Rabies and Kennel Cough and for cat vaccination including 4 in 1, Rabies and FeLV Different types of products for external and internal parasites control are available too

 

MICROCHIPPING

Microchipping service is available for pets identification

DENTAL CARE

Dental procedures are able to perform in our clinics including dental scaling & polishing, dental X-ray as well as dental care products

PET BOARDING

As we are a cat friendly clinic, the boarding places for cat and dog are separated to minimise the stress for cat especially
